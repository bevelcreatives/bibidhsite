About Us

Bevel Creatives is a Game Development & Recovery Studio from Nepal founded by Bibidh Gautam & Kaustuv Kumar Batajoo, specialized in rejuvenating struggling games of passionate developers.

Founded by young and passionate game development freelancers, our expert team dives deep into your game's mechanics, player behavior, and monetization strategies to determine and execute the steps needed to turn things around.

We recruited a team of very young, innovative and brilliant minds & trained them in one specialized field each — making us technically brilliant and uniquely suited to understand modern game trends catered towards teenagers.

No upfront, no fees: Only a share of the growth we create.
